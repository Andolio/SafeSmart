About

What is Safe-Smart?

Utilizing an old smartphone, this mechanism allows for a cheaper, safer, and overall smarter way of having home security.

How does it work?

Using motion sensor technology, the Model A-UNO detects motion and almost instantly snaps a picture of them and uploads it to Facebook Messenger.

Why choose Safe-Smart?

Safe Smart is a small, efficient, and practical way to protect your home inexpensively by reusing an old smartphone.

What Demand?

With over 2.5 million home invasions and loss of 4.6 billion dollars in lost property a year in the United States, a mechanism that provides home security has never been more practical for every American.
